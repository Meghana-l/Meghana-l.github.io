About

I’m a Business Analytics graduate student at the University of New Haven, with a background in Information Science Engineering and a deep interest in solving real-world problems through data. I enjoy working at the intersection of logic and creativity—digging into datasets, uncovering patterns, and turning raw information into visuals or reports that actually drive decisions.

Along the way, I’ve built up experience with tools like Power BI, SQL, Excel, and Tableau. I’ve also worked extensively with Visual Studio 2022 to design and run ETL packages, and I’ve used Microsoft SQL Server and SSMS for writing queries and managing data warehouses. These skills help me go beyond just analysis—I can structure data pipelines, clean and transform datasets, and create dashboards that make sense to any audience.

Outside academics, I’ve been involved in campus life in more hands-on ways and contributing to student engagement activities. It’s this mix of data, design, and communication that I really enjoy bringing into every project I work on.
