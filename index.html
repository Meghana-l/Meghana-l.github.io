02 What I do

What
I Do?

What I do

I bring AI into everyday work. I look for the slow, manual, repetitive parts of a business and use data and AI to make them faster, smarter, and a little less painful.

How I do it

I start with the real problem, then reach for the right tool. Sometimes that is SQL and a clean dashboard. Often it is an AI workflow, a RAG app, or an automation that quietly does the work in the background.

Why it matters

AI is not a buzzword to me. It is how I think. I want teams to spend less time on busywork and more time on the decisions that actually move things forward.
